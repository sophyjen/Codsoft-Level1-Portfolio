About Me

Hi! I'm Sophia Jenitta, an enthusiastic and detail-oriented educator turned aspiring Full Stack Developer. With over 9 years of experience in teaching IT and Mathematics based on the Cambridge curriculum, I’ve led both students and teachers with passion and precision. As the Head of IT and Math Department at The Lords' International School, I’ve guided students through a dynamic academic journey while developing digital solutions to enhance their learning.

During my career, I adapted to online teaching quickly, leading the integration of technology using Google Education tools and facilitating remote learning during the pandemic. Beyond teaching, I managed exam operations for eight years, coordinating CAIE exams with the utmost care and accuracy.

Currently, I am pursuing a career shift into Full Stack Development, exploring the latest web technologies. My portfolio includes projects such as a Nostra e-commerce clone, Greenden landing page clone, and a dynamic student form application built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS. With my natural ability to teach, love for coding, and knack for problem-solving, I aim to create user-friendly web applications that bridge technology and education.
